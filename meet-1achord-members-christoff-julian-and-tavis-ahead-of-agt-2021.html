1AChord AGT

1AChord at AGT is a trio of three American singers.

Christoff Hariston, Julian Broddy, and Travis Cunningham are the 1AChord members.

Actually, the young lads are the students of the University of North Carolina Greensboro. Interestingly, the three met at a cafeteria in their college just a month and a half before their AGT performance.

Moreover, it took no time for the boys to have a great bond between them. The musical connection soon turned into a musical group.

No doubt, they will do nothing but amaze us at the AGT’s premiere on June 1, 2021.

Meet 1AChord’s Christoff Hairston

1AChord’s Christoff Hairston is from Greensboro, North Carolina.

His age appears just over 20 years old. However, his exact birth is still under the radars. 

Actually, Hairston attended Weaver Academy in his hometown. Also, he is a freshman majoring in music at UNCG.

Besides, the AGT contestant has not revealed his parents and girlfriend to the public yet. Surely, we will know more about his personal life as he progresses through different stages at AGT.

It seems like Hairston is not on Instagram. However, he has a YouTube channel where he uploads his musical performances.

Get To Know 1AChord’s Julian Broddy

1AChord’s Julian Broddy is from High Point, North Carolina.

Also, Broddy’s age appears to be between 20-25 years old. The AGT performer also not revealed his birth details as of now.

Meet Tavis Cunningham From 1AChord

1AChord’s Tavis Cunningham is a native of Raeford, North Carolina.

Tavis’s age seems to be above 20 years old. In fact, the AGT contestent is a freshman at UNCG.

In addition, he is majoring in musical theatre in his college.

However, there is no information about Tavis’s parents and girlfriend too.

Also, Tavis apparently does not use Instagram and any other social media currently.
