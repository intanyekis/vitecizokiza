Regarding his education, Kelly Paul Oubre Jr. attended Edward Hynes Elementary School (now known as Hynes Charter School). He played for three Milne Boys Home (now known as New Orleans Recreation Development Commission) basketball teams at the time. He also attended George Bush High School in Fort Bend, Texas before transferring to Findlay Prep in Henderson, Nevada for his senior season.

Kelly Paul Oubre Jr. later spent a year at the University of Kansas (2014–15) where he played one season of college basketball before being selected by the Atlanta Hawks. Oubre has played for the Phoenix Suns and Golden State Warriors. He signed with the Charlotte Hornets on August 7, 2021.
