Sexyy Red and Sukihana Address Khia Calling New Women Rappers 'H*es'

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  588 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Khia had a fiery rant this week about the new crop of women rappers being “h*es.” Sexyy Red and Sukihana stood up for their peers and fired back at the “My Neck, My Back” rapper.

“Just another ol washed up [hag] hating ona yung turnt rich [bi**h],” the “SkeeYee” artist wrote in the comments of The Neighborhood Talk’s post about Khia’s rant. “Hatin on [bi**hes] den & you still ah mad hater.”

The “Hood Rats” artist took things a step further and offered to throw hands with the 46-year-old rapper. “I will knock Khia [the f**k] out,” she wrote on X, formerly known as Twitter, on Friday (Oct. 27). In another tweet, she boasted about how she is too busy to hate on someone else. “SUKIHANA is SUKIHANA a has been is a has been,” she wrote. “I’m booked every weekend until next September of NEXT YEAR. I get it I’m ratchet but I live in my truth. Atlease I don’t sit in front of a library mad at Trina everyday with 172 teeth in my mouth.” Check out Red and Sukihana’s comments below.

Khia seemingly had a lot on her mind and decided to go off earlier this week. “I’m ’bout tired of y’all comparing me to these h*es,” she said in a video. “I said, ‘My neck, my back, my pu**y, and my crack.’ Not these ni**as out here, these hoes out here… I still stood 10 toes down like a motherf**king queen. Hair still wrapped like the first day.”

She continued, distinguishing herself from current women rappers by saying, “I ain’t changed a bit, okay. It’s respect me. It’s snatch the motherf**king cat back. It’s don’t trust no motherf**king ni**a. Get your own sh*t. It’s tell that ni**a to hit the motherf**king door. You wanna f**k these bi**hes, you wanna f**k these h*es. It ain’t eat no ni**a’s a** and suck no ni**a’s toes.” Khia asserted that the current women rappers couldn’t have been listening to her because she was not as promiscuous and giving to men as she perceived them to be.

She made references to the recent sex tape that was seen on Sexyy Red’s Instagram story, leading many to believe that it was her in the video. “I been out here 25 years and y’all ain’t seen no pictures of my pu**y, no videos of me sucking d**k, my phone never been stolen and y’all ain’t seen me with no man, bi**h,” she said. “And I got a motherf**king husband. Y’all ain’t never seen me do sh*t. Y’all ain’t seen no husband, no children, no ni**a I’m f**king… Y’all ain’t never seen me twerking, y’all ain’t seen my pu**yhole from the back… Y’all ain’t got sh*t on the queen.”

Sexyy Red denied purposely posting the video via a tweet back earlier this month. “I’m so heartbroken,” she wrote. “Anybody that know me knows I wouldn’t do no goofy [sh*t] like that.”
