The Cast Of Netflix's New Dating Show, Perfect Match, Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on June 29, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1056 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Valentine’s Day this year will be explosive, with a massive reality star cast coming on Netflix for the show - Perfect Match.

Netflix is uniting singles from various reality shows to compete in a new dating game. The hot stars will be pitted against each other in a tropical paradise, with emotions soaring high and one couple beating all odds to win the show. There is a wicked twist to the dating game when potential couples will be split up to form new bonds with arriving singles.

Nick Lacey is set to host the amorous star cast in the love villa, and only one couple is entitled to win. Season one comes with twelve episodes clocking an hour each. Four episodes will drop on Valentine's Day, i.e., February 14th, the following four will air on the 21st, and the final on the 28th.

Twenty-three celebrity singles from reality shows like Love is Blind, The Circle, Too Hot to Handle, Selling Tampa, and Sexy Beasts are set to find love within three weeks. Here's how some of them rank up, based on net worth.

10 Calvin Crooks (The Circle) - $300,000

The software engineer by profession was a contestant on the reality show The Circle. He competed against ten strangers for prize money worth $100,000 but did not win. His alternative career is working as a private chef and athlete. The primary source of his income comes from appearing in reality tv shows and working as a software engineer in the UK. Calvin Crooks' net worth is around $300,000.

9 Diamond Jack (Love Is Blind) - $300,000

The former NBA dancer represented the Atlanta Hawks as a cheerleader and a trained ballet dancer. In February 2020, Diamond Jack debuted on the reality show Love is Blind but did not end up finding love. She also holds the position of chairwoman in the cancer support organization Charged Up. Diamond's net worth is estimated to be around $300,000.

8 Chloe Veitch (The Circle) - $400,000

Chloe Veitch appeared on the Netflix reality dating show Too Hot To Handle for season 2 but had to return home without finding love. The Circle season 2 premiered in January 2020, and Chloe again competed for the prize money of $100,000.

Unfortunately, she did not win, and the reality star is back to try her luck with Perfect Match. She is a successful model and social influencer with a fan following of 1.7 million. The now single beauty split from her actor boyfriend, Jody Latham, in July 2022. Chloe Veitch's net worth is around $400,000.

7 Georgia Hassarati (Too Hot To Handle) - $500,000

The Australian midwife earns around $81,000 per annum and is also a successful social media model and reality star. She rose to fame with the reality show Too Hot to Handle and occasionally does brand endorsements on social media. Dyson Hair, Vitable Vitamins, The Kind Collective, and Lounge underwear are some of the fashion brands promoted by her. Georgia Hassarati has a net worth of $500,000.

6 Colony Reeves (Selling Tampa) - $700,000

Colony Reeves started working as a licensed realtor for Century 21 Beggins Enterprises in 2017. Shifted base to Allure Realty in 2019, she earns around 3% commission from multi-million dollar sales of homes in the Tampa area. Her success was chronicled on the Netflix original reality show Selling Tampa (2021). She is also the founder of Colony Reeves Group, an affluent real estate agency. Colony Reeves has an estimated net worth of $700,000.

5 Anne Sophie Petit Frere (Selling Tampa) - $1 Million

She appeared as one of the youngest cast members on Netflix’s original reality show Selling Tampa (2021). The single reality star is one of the top agents working for Allure. The real estate agent earns around $200,000 per sale commission. She started her career as an office assistant in 2012 and became a licensed realtor for Allure Realty, Tampa, Florida. Anne Sophie Petit Frere's net worth is estimated to be around $1 million.

4 Damian Powers (Love Is Blind) - $1-3 Million

Damian Powers became a well-known face after a successful stint on Netflix’s reality show Love is Blind season one (2020). Before debuting on television, Damian worked as an operational manager in a Five-star enterprise(2004-2017), he later got promoted to the position of general manager (2017-19). Currently, he works as a market manager. His net worth is estimated to be between $1 million to $3 million.

3 Savannah Palacio (The Circle) - $1 Million

Savannah Palacio works as a data researcher on Capitol Hill. Her primary source of earnings is through promoting fashion, beauty, and wellness products on social media. She enjoys a large following on TikTok (600k+), Instagram (405k+), and 18k subscribers on YouTube. Savannah Palacio's net worth is estimated to be between $1-5 million, with some sources pinning her at more than $4 million.

2 Joey Sasso (The Circle) - $1.5 Million

An actor by profession, Joey Sasso rose to fame by starring in the 2015 horror flick Sinister. The film grossed $87.7 million at the box office globally. He has also done a television stint by appearing in the longest sitcom General Hospital for a few episodes. Joey debuted on The Circle (2020) and won the first season. He took home the prize money of $100,000. Joey Sasso's net worth is around $1.5 million.

1 Francesca Farago (Too Hot To Handle) - $3 Million

Francesca Farago's earnings are mainly through social media for promoting fashion and beauty brands to her 5.7 million strong followers. She earned approximately $940,036 for posting 82 product endorsement ads in 2021. After this, she launched a sustainable beachwear collection - Farago The Label. She reportedly earns $86,000 per year via YouTube, and the reality star’s popularity has soared after appearing on the reality series Too Hot to Handle (2020).
