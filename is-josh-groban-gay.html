Is Josh Groban Gay?

No, Josh Groban is not gay. Long-running unfounded suspicions regarding Groban’s sexual orientation were put to rest when he discussed it in an interview with the online publication ETonline.

“If I was gonna be gay, that would’ve been the moment. That was probably my greatest test, and I failed,” the musician, 42, recalls collaborating with Ryan Gosling in the 2011 love film Crazy, Stupid, Love.

In a recent interview with ETonline writer Chris Azzopardi for PrideSource, Groban talks about the ongoing suspicions regarding his sexuality.

“Honestly, if I were gay, I would have had no problem saying it from day one. … People have speculated, have wanted to speculate — whatever, fine. I’m not gay, but if I were, I don’t view it as a bad thing.”
