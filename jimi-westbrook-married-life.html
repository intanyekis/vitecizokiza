Jimi Westbrook’s Personal Life

The 47 years old Jimi Westbrook is a married man. He shared the marital vows with his long-time girlfriend turned wife Karen Fairchild.

By profession, his wife is a country singer who formed the group Little Big Town along with her partner, Phillip Sweet and Kimberly Schlapman in 1998.

Back to their first meeting, the two came face to face with while attending Samford University. Together, they began their music career and joined the band Little Big Town which was followed by Phillip Sweet.

Wedding Ceremony

As of now, the husband and wife are living a happy married life. The two also attended several events, award ceremony and has also performed together.
