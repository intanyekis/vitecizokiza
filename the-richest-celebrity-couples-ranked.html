The Richest Celebrity Couples, Ranked

 Posted on June 29, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1262 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Highlights

  • Celebrity couples possess immense wealth, often doubling or tripling their individual fortunes, thanks to their combined net worth.
  • From Hollywood powerhouses like Salma Hayek and François-Henri Pinault to media mogul Oprah Winfrey and her partner Stedman Graham, these power couples have amassed billions.
  • While Beyoncé and Jay-Z and David and Victoria Beckham rank lower on the list, they still have substantial wealth, further solidifying their status as iconic celebrity couples.

It goes without saying that by and large, celebrities have lots of money. As a celebrity couple though, that money doubles, triples or grows exponentially – allowing many celebrity couples grow their combined wealth significantly.

At a surface level, celebrities have money due to the various projects they take on, and most celebrities diversify their income into investments after making a name for themselves (as is often the case for individuals with high net worths). Since most celebrities get married to other celebrities, their combined net worth increases significantly.

Let's take a look at the richest celebrity couples ranked, based on their combined net worth.

1 Salma Hayek And François-Henri Pinault: $7.2 Billion

Salma Hayek and François-Henri Pinault have been together since 2006, and got married in 2009. Salma is known for roles in the film industry as an actress and film producer.

She started her career as a telenovela star, and has gone on to take many diverse and well paid roles since then. François-Henri Pinault on the other hand, is a French billionaire and well known business executive with various successful ventures – among them, being the founder of the luxury group Kering and the investment holding company Artemis.

At a surface level, the two both have significant values; combined, their fortune is estimated to be about $7.2 billion, making them first on the list.

2 Steven Spielberg And Kate Capshaw: $5.4 Billion

The Academy Award winner director made his breakout when the film Jaws premiered in 1975, and has since gone on to enjoy a lofty career in directing – making billions in the process. Steven Spielberg got married to Kate Capshaw, an actor in 1991 and ever since have grown their wealth to about $5.4 billion.

As such, Spielberg and Capshaw rank as the second-richest couple in 2023.

3 Oprah Winfrey And Stedman Graham: $3.51 Billion

Media mogul Oprah Winfrey and her partner of 32 years, Stedman Graham, met in 1986 and have been together ever since. Winfrey has been known for her namesake show and her Golden Globes Speech – and has even been brought up in talks for running for the presidency at some point (a move her partner openly supported).

On the other hand, Stedman is an American author, educator and a public speaker. The couple is the blueprint for a signature power couple, and have a combined net worth of $3.51 billion, landing them at third on the list.

4 Holly Valance And Nick Candy: $2.20 Billion

The Australian-British actress and tv presenter Holly Valence started her career way back in 1999, and was for a while actively engaged in various TV shows and movies. In 2012, she got married to property tycoon Nick Candy, a well known luxury real estate developer.

Nick Candy and his brother Christian are among the richest property developers in Britain, having featured on multiple well known projects. Valance and Candy got married, and have since accrued a net worth of $2.2 billion – placing them at fourth on the list.

5 Beyoncé And Jay-Z: $1.5 Billion

Beyoncé and Jay-Z are probably the most notable couple on this list, and the first to come to mind when thinking of celebrity power couples. Beyoncé is a chart topping Grammy Award winner, having bagged 28 of the awards, and she has become an icon.

Jay-Z's career is decades long, and spans across the music industry to various business investments. The two have a combined value of $1.5 billion, legions of fans between them, a strong media presence, and they rank fifth on the list.

6 David And Victoria Beckham: $800 Million

It's well known that David Beckham is among the richest retired athletes in the world. He was watched the world over when playing for multiple soccer clubs in England, the United States, and Europe, setting multiple records and being excellent at football the entire time.

Beckham also launched his company (DB Ventures) and was part of a project in the expansion of the major league soccer team Inter Miami, and Victoria Beckham is known for pop career and luxury fashion vibes.

The two have a combined net worth of about $800 Million (steadily on the rise), but for the moment rank sixth on the list.

7 George And Amal Clooney: $550 Million

George Clooney is a well known American actor, who is married to Amal Clooney, a noteworthy Lebanese lawyer based in London. They tied the knot back in 2014 and their combined net worth rose to $550 million.

Both parties made names for themselves in their individual fields before uniting to become a power couple of their own, making them seventh on the list.

8 Elton John And David Furnish: $548 Million

After nearly 30 years of being together, Elton John and David Furnish are somewhat the face of the same-sex marriages. The two met in October 1993 and have been together ever since.

Their journey is one to inspire any couple – having met during trying times, they helped each other out and have been rock steady ever since. Elton is known as a remarkable singer and performer, and as an English rock legend. David Furnish is, on the other hand, a Canadian former filmmaker.

The two have a combined net worth of $548 million – a figure that ranks them eighth on the list.

9 Jennifer Lopez And Ben Affleck: $546 Million

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck have been in the spotlight for years, famously breaking up before rekindling their relationship and getting married in August 2022. Jennifer is known for her roles as an actor and singer, while Affleck hit it big in Hollywood after his role in the film Good Will Hunting.

The two have a rather unorthodox relationship, which comes with a combined value of $546 million – placing them at ninth on this list.

10 Sting And Trudie Styler: $545 Million

Sting reigns as one of rock and roll's enduring heartthrobs. Having been together with his wife Trudie Styler for 30 years, just recently celebrating their anniversary, the two have rounded up a combined value of $545 over the years, closing the list at tenth.

The couple are parents to four kids and are a longstanding rock and roll dream couple.
