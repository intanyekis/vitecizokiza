Folashodun Adebisi Shonubi: A Biography of the CBN Governor, His Age, Career, and Net Worth

Folashodun Adebisi Shonubi is a renowned Nigerian banker who currently holds the position of Governor at the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN).

His appointment, announced on June 9th, 2023, signifies his succession of the recently suspended Godwin Emefiele, who had been in the role since 2014.

Folashodun Adebisi Shonubi: A Brief Profile

Folashodun Adebisi was born on March 7, 1962. He completed his Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Lagos between 1978 and 1983. He continued his education at the same institution, obtaining a Master of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in Production Engineering, in 1985. Subsequently, from 1988 to 1989, he pursued a Master’s degree in Business Administration with a focus in Finance, expanding his knowledge and expertise in Finance.

Age of Folashodun Adebisi Shonubi

Currently, Folashodun Adebisi Shonubi is 61 years old.

Acting as CBN Governor

Upon the suspension of Godwin Emefiele as the CBN governor, Shonubi was appointed as the acting head of the apex bank.

The Career of Folashodun Adebisi Shonubi

His professional journey commenced as a Consultant Engineer at Mek-ind Associates, and he subsequently held positions such as Marketing Executive at Inlaks Computers Limited, Head of Treasury Operations at Citibank Nigeria Limited, and Deputy General Manager at MBC International Limited. He also served in various leadership roles at organizations including First City Monument Bank (FCMB) Limited, Ecobank Nigeria Plc, Renaissance Securities Nigeria Limited, and Union Bank of Nigeria Limited.

Following his diverse career trajectory, he assumed the role of Managing Director/CEO at Nigeria Inter-Bank Settlement System Plc and later joined the Central Bank of Nigeria as Deputy Governor, Operations, in October 2018. He has also acted as the CBN Governor’s representative on the Board of FIRS (Federal Inland Revenue Service) since December 9, 2019. His latest achievement includes his appointment as the Acting Governor of the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) on June 9, 2023.

Personal Life

Folashodun Adebisi Shonubi enjoys a blissful married life along with his children.
