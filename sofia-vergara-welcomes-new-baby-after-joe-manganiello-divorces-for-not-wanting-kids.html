Sofia Vergara Welcomes New Baby Into Family After Joe Manganiello Divorces Her For Not Wanting Kids

Highlights

  • Sofia Vergara revealed a new addition to her family on social media, showing a tender moment with a family member cradling a newborn.
  • Though Alfonso isn't Sofia's son, this isn't the first time he has been featured on her social media, indicating a close relationship.
  • Sofia confirmed that she didn't want more children with her ex-husband, and she is open to love again but not having any more kids. She seemingly struggles with the idea of her ex-husband starting a family with someone else.

Sofia Vergara may not have wanted any more children of her own, which reportedly led to her divorce from Joe Manganiello last year, but there is a new baby in her immediate family, which she happily revealed on social media.

On Thursday, a heartwarming snapshot emerged on Sofia's social media account, showcasing a tender moment involving her family member, Alfonso Vergara, cradling his newborn while seated in a cozy chair. It’s unclear how the two are related, however, this isn’t the first time Alfonso has been featured on the Modern Family star’s social media, suggesting they have a close relationship.

Sofia Showcased Her New Family Member On Social Media

Sofia, 51, adorned her post with the endearing caption, "New Vergara," accompanied by a vibrant red heart and a celebratory emoji.

Though Alfonso isn’t Sofia’s son, she does have one child who could potentially make her a grandmother in the near future. Sofia shares her 32-year-old son, Manolo, with her former spouse, Joe Gonzalez. They were married from 1991 to 1993, before Sofia made her mark in Hollywood.

But the actress went through an even more recent divorce last year when Joe Manganiello filed to dissolve their marriage after they tied the knot eight years earlier. The divorce was settled quickly and the exes claimed in a statement it was amicable, though various reports have surfaced claiming that Joe changed his mind on watning kids, which Sofia was adamant about not wanting more.

Sofia Confirmed She Didn’t Want Babies With Her Ex-Husband

Sofia seemed to confirm the speculation last month while speaking to Spanish newspaper El País, saying, “Well, I’m newly divorced from my second husband, who I was with for 10 years. My marriage broke up because my husband was younger. He wanted to have kids and I didn’t want to be an old mom.”

Despite the recent divorce, Sofia said she’s open to love again (though don’t expect her to have anymore kids). “So, if love comes along, he has to come with [his own] children,” the actress went on. “I’m almost in menopause, it’s the natural way of things. When my son becomes a dad, let him bring the baby to me for a while and then I’ll give it back to him and go on with my life. That’s what I have to do.”

Both Joe and Sofia have been on other dates with people since their divorce news broke, with Sofia romancing a doctor at the beginning of the year, though it’s seemingly fizzled out. However, the actress is reportedly struggling to see Joe move on so quickly, especially thinking of him having a baby with someone else.

“Joe’s not trying to rub it in Sofía’s face. But he does feel like enough time has passed,” a source told OK! Magazine. “She’s clearly still upset. Especially now that Joe’s publicly showing off his new, younger girlfriend.”
