Courteney Cox is giving Friends fans all the feels with her latest Instagram post, sending them on a trip down memory lane, and drawing parallels between herself and Monica Gellar.

It all started when she set up a fake tennis match between CC and CeCe. Loyal fans know that these are the two nicknames she has for herself, and it quickly became apparent that this fun and sarcastic video depicted Courteney Cox in a match against... herself! Fans just can't separate her identity from that of Monica Gellar, and there were many Friends episodes that quickly flooded the minds of her fans.

The Fabulously Funny Video

The video itself was as funny as it gets. Courteney hit the court with an epic matchup against her alter ego.

One version of herself, seemingly the 'Monica,' was very confident and seemed ready to slam the tennis ball with ferociousness.

The other version of Cox appeared visibly stressed, yet determined, and her face was covered in beads of sweat as she gave this match her all.

This was all set to the sounds of The Eye Of The Tiger, and the build up was epic. The ball was whipped and the competition was very real.

So were the nostalgic Friends memories that came rushing back...
