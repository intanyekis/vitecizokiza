Chick-Fil-A Sells Heart-Shaped Trays for Valentine's Day

 Posted on May 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  240 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Boxes of chocolate are so last year and, let’s be real, candy hearts kind of taste like chalk. This Valentine’s Day, show your loved ones how you really feel with a heart-shaped box of chicken.

Yes, chicken. Chick-fil-A has got you covered, offering heart-shaped trays of their signature 30-count Chick-fil-A Nuggets and 10-count Chick-n-Minis in honor of the oft-hated holiday. Because nothing says ‘I love you’ like crispy fried chicken and biscuits, right?

But before you get too excited, we have some good news and some bad news. We’ll start with the bad news: This promotion is only available at select locations, so you should check with your local CFA before stopping in. The good news? The heart-shaped trays are already available at those locations, so you can get a head start on Valentine’s and win all the bonus points right after you finish reading this.

The heart-shaped trays full of nuggets and minis cost the same price as they would without the novelty containers—in New York the 30-count nuggets will set you back $17.69 and the 10-count Chick-n-Minis will cost you $12.19, though prices vary by market. The promotion will run through February 28, or while supplies last.
