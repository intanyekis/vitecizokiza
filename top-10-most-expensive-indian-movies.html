Indian Film Industry has many records and there are thousands of movies produced annually under many languages.  Indian film covers many languages and has huge audiences. Since big budget movies is the key to success. Here's look of some Indian films with very high budgets:

10. Mughal-E-Azam,  $10.5  Million

Mughal-E-Azam is a 1960 Indian  "period epic " film directed by late k. Asif ,  starring Prithvi Raj Kapoor, Dilip Kumar, Madhubala in a pivotal role.  It has become the highest grossing Indian Film of all time.  Film was also won the  "National Film Award".

9. Ghajini,  $12 Million

Ghajini is a 2008 Indian  "action psychological thriller"  film written and directed by A.R Murugadoss,  starring Aamir khan and Asin in the lead role.  The Film was inspired by the Amercian film  "memento".  Aamir khan's character was featured in a 3d video game titled "Ghajini-The Game", which is based on the Movie.

8.  Love Story 2050,  $12.7 Million

Love Story 2050 is a 2008 Indian  "science fiction"  movie starring Harman Baweja and Priyanka Chopra , directed by Harry Baweja.  The Film failed to generate a big initial response and just crashed in two week.

7.  Ek Tha Tiger,  $13 Million

Ek Tha Tiger is a 2012 Indian  "action"  movie,  directed by Kabir Khan and produced by Yash raj films , starring Salman Khan and Katrina kaif in the lead role. This Film became the "second highest-grossing"  Indian Film of all time.

6. Don 2, $14 Million

Don 2 is a 2011 Indian  "action thriller"  directed by Farhan Akhter , starring Shahrukh Khan and Priyanka Chopra. It is sequel of the movie "don". The Film fetched several awards at the popular awards ceremonies.

5. Vishwaroop,  $16 Million

Vishwaroop is a 2013  Indian  "spy thriller"  film, written and directed by Kamal hassan who also played the lead role in the movie. The film was be produced in the three languages: Tamil, Telgu and Hindi.  The movie also won the  "National Film Award".

4. Dorna,  $17 Million

Drona is a 2008 Indian  "fantasy adventure superhero"  film, directed by Golde Behl, starring Abhishek Bachchan and Priyanka Chopra. The Producer spent over $2 million on the special effects of this movie and for an imaginary den for the villain. The movie featured Indian Martial Arts.

3. Blue,  $24 Million

Blue is a 2009 Indian  "underwater action thriller"  film, directed by Anthony D'Souza and starring Sanjay Dutt, Akshay Kumar, and Lara Dutta in the lead role with Australian pop singer Kylie Minogue, which appeared in a musical number in the movie, Kylie was paid $1 million for that song. The film is based around a Underwater treasure. Despite all this, the film was declared a flop.

2. Ra-One,  $27 Million

Ra-One is a 2011  "Science Ficton Superhero" film directed by Anubhav Sinha, and starring Shahrukh Khan , Kareena Kapoor in a pivotal role. The film notably won the "National Film Award" and the Filmfare Award for "best special effects" and four  "International Indian film academy awards".

1. Robot, $ 35 Million

Robot is a 2010 Indian "Science Fiction Action"  Film,  co-written and directed by Shankar,  starring Rajnikant and Aishwariya Rai Bachchan in lead role. The film, which was released in Hindi and Tamil,  has been in the making for over two years, and has been shot in exotic location like  "Machu Picchu , Austria, Brazil and the U.S.A."
