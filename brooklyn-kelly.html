Brooklyn Kelly is a New Zealander vlogger, model, and certified nurse from Gold Coast, Australia. She is known for her self-titled YouTube channel and blog, where she shares her travel, fashion, and beauty vlogs. In December 2018, she obtained her nursing degree from Griffith University in South East Queensland, Australia. She has done campaigning for Tendence watches, Valley Eyewear, One Teaspoon, Style Inc, Top Shop, River Island, and others. She has also been featured on Men’s Health magazine. Brooklyn has a huge social media fan base with more than 300k followers on Instagram.
