10 Celeb Couples Who Worked By Keeping Their Relationships Under The Radar

 Posted on June 22, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1172 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The world of Hollywood is filled with bad breakups and heartbreaking splits, but there have been a few A-list couples who have managed to make their relationship work, proving that true love can exist in the limelight.

RELATED: Travis Barker & 9 Other Celebrity Rock Star Boyfriends

So what are these long-lasting celebrity couples' biggest secret on making their relationships work? Well, it seems that keeping their relationship under the radar and extremely private has been one of their best moves yet. Instead of constantly talking about one another to the press and posting a ton of photos on social media, these stars like to remain out of the spotlight as much as they can, and it has proven to work well for these celebs.

10 Dave Franco And Alison Brie

Did anyone know that Dave Franco was a married man? The actor has kept his relationship with actress Alison Brie out of the public eye for some time that most people didn't even realize that they had been together for nearly a decade before tying the knot in 2017.

RELATED: Everything We Know About Sofia Richie's Dating History

Brie admitted that she never really wanted to get married, but that all changed when she met Franco when she shared, "It was not a priority for me and I may have just been fine never getting married except that, you know, I fell madly in love and then thought, 'Oh, I guess I could be married to you.'"

9 Margot Robbie And Tom Ackerley

Actress Margot Robbie has been in several box office hits, but not much is known about her private life with her husband of nearly six years Tom Ackerly. It appears the couple likes to keep their marriage super low-key, but Ackerly has always been spotted attending red carpet events with his wife.

Like Robbie, Ackerly works in Hollywood as an assistant director and producer who founded his own production company with his wife called LuckyChap Entertainment which has produced movies like I, Tonya, and Hulu's Dollface. 

8 Zoe Saldana And Marco Perego

Actress Zoe Saldana and her husband Marco Perego are happily raising three children together, but fans may not have known that since this Hollywood couple likes to keep their marriage private. The two have been married since 2013 and it looks like not talking about their ups and downs with the press has seemed to work well for them.

RELATED: What Really Happened Between Frank Sinatra And Mia Farrow?

Saldana knew it was love at first sight for her when she shared with Marie Claire, "I don't do the ABCs, I do what my heart says, what my heart feels. So, from the moment I met my husband, we were together. We knew."

7 Zach Braff And Florence Pugh

Zach Braff and Florence Pugh have received a ton of backlash over their relationship because of their substantial age difference. The actor is 21 years older than the Little Woman star, but the two have managed to keep their relationship under wraps and have been together since 2019.

Not many celeb couples with huge age differences have worked out in Hollywood, including former couples like Sofia Richie and Scott Disick, but it looks like the pair are keeping their personal lives way more private than most celebs.

6 Sacha Baron Cohen And Isla Fisher

Comedia Sacha Baron Cohen and actress Isla Fisher have been married since 2011 and they continue to be named as one of the strongest couples in Hollywood. They are also regarded as Hollywood's most private couple and it's definitely worked in their favor.

RELATED: 10 Actors Who Successfully Faked An Accent On-Screen

Fisher even once praised her husband for helping her get into comedy. She shared, "I was losing confidence in my abilities when he recommended that I do comedy. He felt I was really funny, so when someone as funny as him recommends that, I listened and actually auditioned for 'Wedding Crashers,' which ended up being my big break."

5 Amanda Seyfried And Thomas Sadoski

Amanda Seyfried and Thomas Sadoski started as costars in the Off-Broadway play The Way We Get By, and both stars were in relationships. While the actress admitted that the two never flirted while with their respective partners, there was chemistry and eventually, both Hollywood actors wound up together.

Seyfried and Sadoski secretly tied the knot in March 2017, while she was nine months pregnant with their daughter, Nina, and in 2020, the two welcomed their second child named after Thomas. They have continued to keep their marriage pretty private and appear to like it that way.

4 Eva Mendes And Ryan Gosling

Eva Mendes and Ryan Gosling have been together since 2011 when they met on the set of the movie The Place Beyond the Pines. Since then, the couple has welcomed two daughters and remains one of the most private people in Hollywood.

Mendes and Gosling don't share much about one another or their children on social media and refrain from making too many public appearances. Surprisingly, the stars have yet to tie the knot, but it appears that they don't need rings on their fingers to define their strong relationship.

3 Emma Roberts And Garrett Hedlund

Actress Emma Roberts has been dating her boyfriend Garrett Hedlund for over a year now and the two recently welcomed her first child, a boy named Rhodes Robert Hedlund. However, the two have remained quiet on their relationship and have appeared on Roberts' Instagram just a handful of times.

While on the cover of Cosmopolitan, the actress opened up about her private life with Hedlund and her pregnancy journey, sharing, "Things can go wrong when you're pregnant. That's something you don't see on Instagram. So I kept it to myself, my family, my partner, not wanting to make grand plans if it wasn't going to work out."

2 Penelope Cruz And Javier Bardem

One star-studded couple most fans don't usually hear about in the press is Penelope Cruz and Javier Bardem The couple began dating in 2007 but kept their romance quiet until the actor gushed about Cruz during an award acceptance speech, stating, "I share this joy with my friend, my companion, my love: Penelope."

The pair got hitched in 2010 and continue to live their lives out of the public eye, with Cruz once sharing with Oprah Daily, "It was a really good decision for us, not to talk about our relationship. It would feel very strange to do it a different way. I just couldn't do it."

1 Joshua Jackson And Jodie Turner-Smith

One of the loveliest relationships to come out of Hollywood is between actor Joshua Jackson and his wife Jodie Turner-Smith. The two apparently met at Usher's 40th birthday bash and couldn't get their eyes off one another.

The couple tied the knot in secret in 2019 and welcomed a daughter on April 21, 2020. While the two keep their romance away from the limelight, once in a while, the duo will share sweet photos of one another on Instagram.
