Who is Zora Ideale?

Zora Ideale is the supporting protagonist of the Japanese Anime and Manga series, Black Clover. Ideale is renowned as the 1st Class Junior Magic Knight of the Clover Kingdom’s Magic Squad, Black Bulls.

The character happens to be the son of Zara Ideale (a peasant who became a magic knight).

Know the Voice Behind Zora Ideale

– The Japanese Voice artist, Hikaru Midorikawa is the Japanese Voice of the character. The Voice Actor from Otawara is currently representing the talent agency, Aoni Production. He is popular for providing a voice to the character, Kaede Rukawa in Slam Dunk and Seiran Shi in Saiunkoku Monogatari.

– The English dubbed voice behind the character is the actor, Johnny Yong Bosch. Bosch is also a Martial Artist, singer, and musician. He is renowned as the black ranger of the Mighty Morphin Power Rangers. 

Zora Ideale- Birth Age, Family Background & Childhood

Zora Ideale was born in the Clover Kingdom as the son of a peasant, Zara Ideale. His birth date is the 19th of December. Currently, he is 25 years old.

His father, Zara was a poor peasant of the Clover Kingdom. Also, he happens to be the first peasant in the entire kingdom who proved himself worthy of being the Magic Knight of the Kingdom. Being the first Magic Knight from a poor family, he proved that your magical abilities are not based on the family that you born.

While growing up, he saw the struggle that his father faced to pass the Magic Knight of the Kingdom. From that age, he already knew that the strong determination within you is the key to success. However, at the early age of 12, he lost his father who was the source of inspiration for many young children who wanted to be the Magic Knight despite born in a common family.

Who killed Zara Ideale?

As Zara belonged to common family background and strong himself. He was an eyesore for the magic knight who is from noble families and couldn’t prove their worth within the country.

Hence, he was betrayed by the noblemen of his squad, Purple Ocra. In the course of saving the commoners of the kingdom, he got killed.

How Zora became the Black Bulls’ Member?

After finding the truth behind his father’s death, his sole mission was to punish the corrupted Magic Knight of the Kingdom. In the course of his mission, he encountered the captain Yami Sukehiro.

After that, Yami offered him a space in his squad. However, he declined the offer. Despite refusing the offers, he went to the Royal Knights Selection Exam and placed his trap magic.

Zora Ideale’s Love Interest

In the series, the character is portrayed as a lonely character who is just after his sole mission to punish the corrected Magic Knight. Currently, he is single. He has not been seen flirting with any other characters.

Similarly, no female character seems interested in him. However, he shares a very strong bond with his squadmates in his own way. In fact, he never shows that he likes his squad.

Zora Ideale- Magical Tools, Abilities, Spells, & Powers

Tools

– Grimoire- He holds the three-leaf clover grimoire that consists of the ash-based magic spells.

– Rainbow Stink Bug- He possesses a Rainbow Strink Bug that holds seven different colors and has the smelliest stink.

Magical Powers

– Ash Magic- He possesses the Ash magic that allows him to generate and manipulate ash.

– Trap Magic- Besides the Ash magic, he is able to use the Trap Magic that allows him to set various magical traps. However, he takes more time to set the complicated and powerful traps.

Abilities

His abilities include enhanced speed, endurance, keen intelligence, and large Magic Power.
