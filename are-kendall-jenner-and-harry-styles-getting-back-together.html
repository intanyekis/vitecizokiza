Is Kendall Jenner Going After Harry Styles After Dumping Devon Booker?

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  904 words  |  Martina Birk

Harry Styles has been in a very public relationship with Olivia Wilde until late. After the two "very amicably" called things off, both took solace with friends. It just so happens that one of those friends for Styles was his ex, Kendall Jenner. And as people will do when they see celebrities together who once dated, rumors began to fly that Jenner and the Watermelon Sugar singer were getting back together.To add fuel to the fire, the Keeping Up With The Kardashians star broke up with her boyfriend, Devon Booker, recently as well. This has left "more time" for Jenner and Styles to spend time together. And while Jenner's camp has claimed that the two just have a friendly relationship and Styles is using the term "taking a break" rather than using the word breakup, fans still believe that there is a chance that the two could rekindle the old flame between them. As such, any public outing where the two are spotted together or in the same vicinity becomes fodder to stoke the relationship rumors.

Kendall Jenner And Devon Booker Break Up Nearly Coincided With Harry Styles And Olivia Wilde's "Break"

After news broke that Styles and Wilde were no longer together, stories began to make their way to the media that Jenner and Booker had also gone their separate ways with their relationship.

Neither couple had anything bad to say about the other. Instead, publicists gave the same reason. And that reason was that everyone simply had too much professionally going on.

Though there was not any huge scandal that caused the relationships to end, because they did, fans have been looking to see who the next person is that their favorite celebrities would be hanging out with.

For Wilde, it was her girlfriends. Booker has been quiet since the relationship ended. But for Jenner and Styles, they have been keeping company with one another. And this had caused some to call the timing of the breakups to be more than "coincidental."

"Kendall Jenner and Devon Booker broke up around the time Harry Styles and Olivia Wilde broke up, why am I smelling a Harry Kendall comeback!!" one fan exclaimed. Others are banking on a 2023 reunion between the two. Only time will reveal if they are right.

Kendall Jenner And Harry Styles Have Provided A "Shoulder To Cry On" For One Another

Given the history between the two celebrities, it would not be unheard of for the former couple to talk with one another to gain perspective on their situations or to receive some comfort. And with the exes providing a "shoulder to cry on," fans believe there may be more going on than meets the eye.

While Jenner and the As It Was singer were on-again and off-again from 2013 to 2016, they have managed to remain "close" since going their separate directions. So close in fact, that after breakups, the two have been known to "go away together."

And given the speculation that the two may "hang out over the holidays," the rumors that the two are nothing more than friends are not going anywhere soon.

Fans Things Harry Styles Blew A Kiss At Kendall Jenner During A Recent Concert

To add fuel to the relationship fire was the interaction between Styles and the model at the singer's recent concert.

While it would not be unheard of for Jenner and her friends to support the Music for a Sushi Restaurant singer, a kiss blown from the stage occurred during a very surprising moment, leaving fans' tongues wagging about what exactly was happening between Styles and Jenner.

During Styles' mid-November concert, he performed Love of My Life. Not an unexpected choice, given that it is from his most recent album.

What caused a commotion among fans was that Styles blew a kiss, seemingly to Jenner during this song. All of which led to comments ranging from, "WHAT!!" to "shut up they're getting back together."

Perhaps with their close relationship, Styles and Jenner may very well love one another. Perhaps the song is about Jenner herself. Whether that love is platonic or romantic is all in the eyes of the beholder.

Harry Styles And Kendall Jenner May Just Be Better Off As Friends

Given that Jenner and Styles just got out of two very serious relationships, it stands to reason that the two are spending time together just as friends. A move to be close to someone after a heartbreak but to keep things uncomplicated by remaining platonic.

Those close to The Kardashians star and the What Makes You Beautiful singer, state that there is "no intention of getting back together." But because the two know each other so well, they are relying on one another during tough times in their lives.

After all, being surrounded by loved ones, especially during the holidays makes dealing with the end of relationships easier.

And because the professional conflicts between traveling for modeling and Tequila 818, as well as traveling to perform music that were there between Jenner and Styles when they were a couple still remain, it makes sense that the two would not be pursuing anything romantic. At least for the time being.
