10 beautiful Bathroom under stairs ideas you can try out in your apartment or home.

 Posted on June 23, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1235 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Bathroom is one of the most essential parts of any building either a house, office, airport, train station, or any public building. 

Bathrooms are essentially located in the bathroom at home but if you have a space under your case, you can easily convert it to a bathroom. 

A bathroom under stairs can serve as a visitor’s toilet or restroom instead of them using the bathroom in your bedroom.

In this piece, we will be sharing some bathroom under stair ideas with you even if you live in a minimalist home. 

Bathroom Under Stairs Ideas 

  • Warm Toilet in Bold Theme under Staircase

A tiny latrine in this light-dark house looks one of a kind. The uniqueness is applied to the striking color shown on its wall. Rather than applying white or brilliant shading on the wall, this bathroom shows dim brown to cover the whole divider aside from the inclining rooftop. 

There is a white hole between the dim earthy colored wall and the dim wooden floor. Furthermore, to light up the divider, a splendid composition is outlined on the middle wall.

From an external perspective, we can see a spotless water storage room with an oval mat before it. It appears to be that the washbasin is joined across the water storage room.

  • Tiny Water Closet Nook under the Stairs

In a bid to convert the normal storage, you can have a small latrine situated under the steps. The latrine doesn’t need to be a standard washroom with a bath or shower cabin.

This little room might include just a moderate latrine and a washbasin for washing necessities. Basic and clean can be the best impression given to the visitors as the clients. Flower-printed tiles are in dim shading. 

These tiles are introduced on the floor. In contrast to the floor, the divider looks cleaner with clay tiles. Those ceramic tiles are organized in block style. A pot of vegetation is put in the corner to keep this dull region seriously invigorating.

  • Narrowed Bathroom under Stairs

This restroom under the steps can also be referred to as a powder room. This room has more expanded space with the slope on the rooftop. Thus, the slope region is covered exquisitely with wood boards. This wood board covering is additionally shown on the wall behind the latrine. 

Two wall sides come as the painted divider. For this situation, the divider is painted in a delicate cream. An interesting hexagon-formed mirror is connected to the divider where a major gliding bowl is installed. 

There is a hanger with a towel hanging close to the entryway. Also, to improve this region, a flower grower is drifting over the latrine.

  • Modern Powder Room under Stairway

This bathroom under stairs is a bit different from the previous examples of washrooms under the steps.  

This powder room comes in a more extensive space. It comprises a two-board swinging entryway. Obviously, every entryway board has an alternate shape since they need to follow the state of the steps. 

Like the entryways, the powder room itself has an asymmetric shape. The region with slope is intentionally utilized for latrine complete with tissue holder connected on the cream tiled divider. 

An advanced free-standing sink is set on the middle wall with a frameless mirror above it. There is no restroom mat covering the wooden birch floor.

  • Shower Cabin under the Stairs

Any house with two staircases standing close to one another should have enough space underneath. Now, this space can be used as a major bathroom for the family. It matches what we can find in the image above. 

There are two slants shown in the restroom. The first one is utilized as a shower lodge, while the other one is used to put an exemplary oval bath. 

The center region with windows turns into the biggest piece of this room, where a latrine and a bureau with twofold mounted sinks are found. A major outlined mirror is joined to the wall where the sinks are found.

  • Stylish Bathroom Under Stairs with Laundry

Most times, the bathroom and washrooms are located in separate areas of the house. A good number of people even tend to have the laundry room separate from the bathroom.

However, this bathroom under the stairs has both the laundry room and the bathroom in the same room since they both serve a similar function of cleaning and washing.

The bathroom is quite big since it can provide room for a shower cabin, washbasin, latrine, repository, and washing machine.

The shower room is located in the corner with a glass door structure. This cabin is between the floating sink with a mirror and a washing machine with a metallic clothes frame.

  • Artistic Dots over the Wall of Bathroom

These days, Wallpaper have become an option to embellish the walls of any room even quicker than paint. This wall art is also used on the wall of the bathroom under stairs. This time, a dots pattern is chosen to make the bathroom attractive. 

The dots wallpaper is obvious in almost the entire part of the bathroom wall except for a small region on the lowest wall.

A distinctive square toilet is installed under the slope. There is a  wall erected that allows people to store some items on it. It is like a wall warehouse in this tiny space.

  • Under Staircase Bathroom with Shower

This new restroom is still built under the staircase. In an unusual triangular shape, this restroom looks even tinier. In fact, when someone comes inside, he or she will realize that it is bigger than they had ever imagined.

There is a large shower compartment with a glass wall and an entrance installed in this room. A free-standing washbasin is positioned in between the shower cabin and the toilet.

Tiles are selected to keep this room carefully selected and installed to ensure that it is easy to clean and maintain. It also has a  bright lantern on the sloping roof which makes this small room futuristic in grey gradation.

  • Chic Slope Bathroom with Window under Staircase

There is a small cabin which is built underneath the staircase. This small room is also known as a powder room or toilet. This bathroom under stairs is unique from the popular bathroom because it only has a toilet and a sink.

This room is always dry because it is only used for washing, having a pee, and poo, especially for the visitors or home guests. In smaller dimensions, this room also needs good ventilation which is why a small window is installed on the wall where a water closet is installed. A floating washbasin looks better than the free-standing one in this case. 

  • Interesting Powder Room with Hexagon Tiles

This fascinating bathroom under stairs will get much attention from a lot of people with its chic appearance if you design it exactly like this. 

It appears as a super narrowed powder room that is situated under the staircase. In asymmetric shape, this narrowed room can only accommodate a small floating toilet and sink.

A wall storage mirror looks perfect for giving extra space for toiletries storage beside the open racks attached above the toilet.

The center wall of this toilet shows the elegance of hexagon tiles. And darker tiles are used on the floor as well without any carpet or rug covering it.
