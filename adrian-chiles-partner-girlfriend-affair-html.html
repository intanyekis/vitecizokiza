In 2010, he moved to ITV network, where he worked as a football presenter until 2015. He returned to BBC Radio 5 Live and currently hosts Chiles on Friday, alongside Emma Barnett.

Who Is Adrian Chilies Alleged Girlfriend? 

Regardless of his successful career, Adrian has faced back-to-back failures in his personal front. He was in a marital relationship with the 54 years old radio presenter, Jane Garvey.

However, the couple announced their separation after ten years of their marriage in 2008. The reason for the bust-up was Adrian's growing career and his inability to turn down the work, which made him stressed and with less time for his wife and two daughters.
