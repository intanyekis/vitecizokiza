Amy Duggar Flashes Bikini Body as She Honeymoons with Dillon King

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Martina Birk

The honeymooners! After their Sept. 6 wedding, Amy Duggar and Dillon King, are on their honeymoon in Mexico — and they're sharing some of the highlights with their fans on social media! In fact, the two debuted the new Mrs. King's bikini body while sharing a video of their swanky suite's patio.

Panning the view from their Cancun getaway's private pool and spa, King, 28, shared a video of flirty Amy, 28, in a strapless aqua two-piece suit. "I like our patio, our private plunge pool is so much more awesome, but my wife rocks even harder," he wrote along with the hashtags #honeymoonkings, #DillandDuggsforever, and #ringsfortheKings. He also mentioned his wife's Instagram handle, which has officially switched over to @amyrachelleking.

The small clip drew lots of comments from fans — many of them positive and congratulatory — with some critiquing Mrs. King's skimpy apparel. "I would like to say congrats to u too [sic]!!!! However, Amy I wish you had a little more modesty," one fan said. "You are a beautiful woman with sooo many little girls watching you. I'm not saying you have to walk around with a trash bag on or wear skirts all the time (because I don't). A lot of young girls would benefit from you being a little more modest in the things you wear and post on IG. No matter how you look at it… a 2 piece bathing suit is a fancy bra/panty set."

But not everyone was upset by the honeymooner's swimwear. Responding to the comment above, another fan defended the newly married Duggar, niece to 19 Kids and Counting's Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar. "Are you serious? She is wearing a bikini on her honeymoon. Women need to stop body-shaming other women! 'Modesty,' as you describe it, is nothing more than a tool for men to control women," the commenter wrote.
