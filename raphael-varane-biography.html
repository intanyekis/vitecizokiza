Who is Raphael Xavier Varane? 

Raphael Varane, also known as Raphael Xavier Varane is a Professional French Footballer.

Currently, he plays as a center-back for France’s National Team and Premier League club Manchester United.

He is called by his nickname “Mr. Clean” as he has barely created any fouls and mistakes. He is an active player in the 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar for France’s national team.
